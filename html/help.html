  • Basics

  • How does SailBoat help me?

    This helps in reducing tab clutter as you can have a lot more tabs open at a time as they are segregated into different tasks. You don’t have to close and lose tabs.

  • How does SailBoat manage Downloads folder?

    A new folder is created for every Task in the Downloads folder and all your downloads are saved there making it much easier to manage your Downloads folder. Goodbye messy Downloads folder!

  • What If I am not doing any tasks , I am doing general browsing?

    If you are not working on any particular task and want to do some general browsing switch to 'Default' task.

  • How do we switch between tasks?

    Click on any Task button in the Dock to switch to that Task. Alternatively you can use shortcut ‘Control+~’ to switch between Tasks.

  • Dock

  • What is SailBoat Dock?

    The Sailboat dock helps you work with Tasks in an easy and quick way. There will be some common Tasks in your dock right when you install it. You can continue to use them or delete them.

  • How do we switch between tasks using Dock?

    Click on any Task button in the Dock to switch to that Task.

  • How do I create a new task?

    Click on the ‘+’ button at the extreme right on the dock. Press ‘enter’ after entering the name of Task.

  • How do I add current tab to any Task?

    Press ‘+’ beside the Task button to add the current tab to that Task

  • How do I view tabs open in a Task?

    Hover on a Task button to see the Tabs open in that Task.

  • How do I archive a tab ?

    Click on the ‘Archive’ button to archive the current page.

  • How do I collapse the dock ?

    Collapse/Expand Dock button is to the extreme left of the Dock.

  • Archived Pages

  • What happens when I archive a page?

    When you archive a page it gives you the ability to search through the full content of the page.So if you find yourself in a position when you sort of remember reading some word somewhere use this feature!

  • Privacy

  • Does SailBoat track my data and history?

    No, it does not. We do not store anything on our servers, it is all stored on your local machine.

  • How do I archive a tab ?

    Click on the ‘Archive’ button to archive the current page.
